# MONA

M.O.N.A (Make Old New Again) is a student-led initiative that reframes electronic waste as accessible infrastructure for hands-on learning.

Instead of treating old electronic devices as useless waste, MONA promotes their reuse as practical environments for experimentation, repair, and technical education. The project encourages a shift from passive consumption of technology to active understanding through building, modifying, and exploring real hardware.

MONA is based on the idea that meaningful technical learning does not require new or expensive equipment — only curiosity and access to existing, often discarded, devices.

Through reuse and experimentation, MONA aims to foster technical awareness, creativity, and environmental responsibility, connecting digital education with real-world systems.
